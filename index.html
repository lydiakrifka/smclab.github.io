About

This is a research-led blog of the Sound and Music Computation master’s programme at Universitat Pompeu Fabra (UPF), Barcelona. Run by Lydia Krifka-Dobes, it documents experiments, projects, and resources around sound, computation, and music technology.

Topics

  • Machine Learning for Sound and Music
  • Sonic Information Retrieval
  • Symbolic Music Analysis
  • Generative Algorithms
  • Sonic Cognition & Perception
